About Us

We are a team of 6 people looking to help people with RAIRDS difficulties and raise awareness about autoimmune diseases, whilst also helping to alleviate the impact these diseases causes in people's life.

How are the calculations done?

Calculations required to calculate Pulse Wave Velocity (PWV):
d = Artery length will need to be measured beforehand (personalisation)
PWV = d / Pulse Transit Time (PTT)

Initial PTT = Time from PPG - Time from ECG
Final PTT = Initial PTT * (1 + C) * f

Some wearables directly give PTT unless they use purely ECG and PPG.
Our calculations factor in movement and posture, so our results are more accurate.

We also use machine learning models to further refine final PTT – to reduce errors from physical activities or environmental factors affecting PWV.

(On average but varies between individuals):
If x is the patient’s PWV:
x < 10 m/s - healthy range
10 m/s < x < 12 m/s - borderline – patient must be alerted
x > 12 m/s - the disease is actively impacting the blood vessels OR there is an error with calculations (e.g., patient is exercising) – patient must be alerted
